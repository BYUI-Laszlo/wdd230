Kőbánya Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Kőbánya Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Kőbánya Chamber of Commerce's goal is to promote businesses, employment, and economic development. To provide promotion of new businesses and to foster the growth and development of our existing ones. To ensure the necessary networking opportunities to further strengthen the business community of our district. Kőbánya Chamber of Commerce takes pride in the cultural, social, and economic support it provides via the diligent work of its members.
