Rule of Thirds

Staak/Studio

https://www.staak.co.uk/

The Rule of Thirds design element is very strong here. Notice how the picture of the bottles take up the upper 2/3rds of the image and the text fills the lower third.

Whitespace

Workleap

https://workleap.com/

On this image there are several elements separated by whitespace. It increases the viewing experience by introducing some breathing space. The eye can rest on one item and move onto another one, giving greater meaning to each of them.

Repetition

Jurassic World

https://www.jurassicworld.com/discover/

The pictures and text elements the fonts and maybe most obviously the buttons show repetition throughout the website. This gives the viewer the feeling that the content elements belong together forming a cohesive whole.
